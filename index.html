About UniExams DZ

Building a better future for Algerian students

UniExams DZ is a student-driven platform dedicated to making past university exam papers accessible to all Algerian students. We believe that quality educational resources should be free and readily available to everyone.

Founded by students, for students, our mission is to create the largest comprehensive archive of university exams across Algeria. We support learning in Arabic, English, and French to cater to all academic backgrounds.

Every exam on our platform is uploaded by the community, ensuring you have access to authentic, high-quality study materials from your peers who have recently taken these courses.
